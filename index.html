IndividualLeasing

The Individual Leasing Program (ILP) is developed to meet the requirements of clients who genuinely wish to lease a vehicle for a reasonably moderate period at a lower monthly rate taking advantage of operating lease benefits.

Whether you prefer leasing or you are unable to buy a car due to high monthly installments, interests or simply do not have a guarantor, ILP is ideal for you. Now, with easy terms, you don't need to worry; simply choose your favorite make and model car and lease it at an affordable rate taking advantage of program benefits and features.

The program provides customers a wide range of brand new cars from Mitsubishi, Chrysler, Dodge, Jeep, Hyundai, Kia and Chevrolet vehicles from small and medium Sedans to Family or Sport Utility 4x4s for an extended period characterized by a down payment and low monthly payments to suit all customers.

This Lease program was developed to meet the requirements of clients wishing to drive their favorite car without necessarily buying it, taking the advantages of special leasing benefits from Comprehensive Insurance, Free Maintenance, Free Replacement, Free Delivery, Breakdown Assistance and many more.
